# **App Name**: Ortiz skin care

## Core Features:

- Luxury Patient Web Experience: Render key clinic content including Homepage, About Us, Services, and Promotions with an ultra-premium, conversion-focused, mobile-first design, adhering to a refined editorial aesthetic with significant whitespace.
- Interactive Branch Locator: Provide a premium, elegant searchable map and detailed information pages for each clinic branch, including contact, operating hours, available services, and map integration, designed with sophisticated layout and spacing.
- Streamlined Appointment Booking: Enable patients to easily select desired services, preferred branch, date, and time slots through a calm, reassuring, and highly premium form layout with minimal distractions.
- Secure Administrative Access: Implement a robust and user-friendly login portal for clinic staff to securely access the backend management system, featuring a premium but minimal design and refined composition.
- Centralized Content Management: Provide an intuitive admin interface for staff to create, edit, and publish content related to services, promotions, branch details, testimonials, and user inquiries, designed for non-technical users with a clean, fast, and simple aesthetic.
- AI-Assisted Content Authoring Tool: A generative AI tool for administrative users to craft or refine marketing copy for services, promotions, and patient communications, ensuring alignment with the exclusive luxury brand tone, medical credibility, and conversion objectives. This tool uses reasoning to decide when to incorporate specific brand information.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Warm Ivory (#F9F7F2) dominating 70% of the interface to establish a foundation of quiet luxury, cleanliness, and expansive whitespace. The background surfaces will be matte and creamy.
- Text Color: Deep Graphite (#2D2D31) used for 18% of the interface, anchoring text and providing premium contrast for excellent readability and a polished, professional aesthetic.
- Supporting Neutral: Soft Stone Taupe (#D9D1C7) comprising 7% of the interface, offering subtle depth and sophistication to complement the dominant ivory and graphite tones.
- Accent Color: Champagne Gold (#D8BC78) used sparingly for 3% of the interface, appearing only in small, precise accents, key dividers, micro-details, and premium CTA highlights to signify exclusivity and luxury.
- Subtle Accent: Muted Berry Dust (#9D858A) comprising 2% of the interface, used as a 'whisper' in subtle elements like pill labels, soft hover states, tiny icon accents, selected tabs, or editorial dividers, never as a dominant section fill.
- Headline Font: 'Playfair' (serif) for commanding, elegant, and editorial headlines, featuring large hero typography, refined spacing, and disciplined line lengths for maximum impact. Body Font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for clean, modern, and highly readable body text. Overall typography emphasizes stronger hierarchy, elegant section intros, and restrained use of bold or uppercase elements.
- Thin line icons only, designed to be minimal, symmetrical, and polished, conveying clarity and luxury without any cuteness, cartoonish softness, or excessive ornamentation, maintaining an Apple-level precision.
- Ultra-clean editorial layout with generous, confident whitespace and expansive margins. The design features fewer but stronger visual elements per section, emphasizing strong hierarchy and a slower, curated rhythm. Tall section spacing, premium card surfaces with faint borders and very soft, deliberate shadows, and refined soft radius corners. Strategic asymmetry is employed for an editorial feel, with reduced card patterns and minimized clutter.
- Subtle and deliberate motion: slow fades, elegant image reveals, gentle opacity transitions, and restrained card hover lifts. Animations are crafted to enhance perceived luxury and calm, avoiding all flashy, sliding, or over-animated effects, focusing on tasteful microinteractions.
